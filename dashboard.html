Key Insights

Strongest first offer: AI Lead Response & Follow-Up System. Highest SMB fit across ease of sale, speed to result, ROI clarity, and upsell potential.
72% use project-based pricing. Fixed-scope engagements dominate. Retainer and subscription models are underrepresented — opportunity for recurring revenue positioning.
Weak players sell abstractions. "AI strategy", "readiness", "transformation" — overused and commoditized. Strong players sell specific pain relief: faster response, fewer losses, less manual work.
51% are small teams (10-50 people). The market is fragmented with many small operators. No dominant player in the SMB automation niche yet.
Dev shops are the largest category (64). Most are offshore generalists adding "AI" to their pitch. True AI-native agencies are a minority — differentiation opportunity.
27 companies have red flags or low evidence. No named clients despite years in business, unverifiable claims, or inflated positioning. Buyers should verify carefully.
