# Agentic AI Agency Leads

Date: April 12, 2026

## Context

- Screened against the full `research.csv` inventory
- Focused on net-new firms not already present in the main CSV
- Excluded shops whose primary positioning is n8n, Make, Zapier, or generic low-code workflow automation
- Filtered for firms selling custom AI agents, multi-agent systems, agent orchestration, tool-using agents, or enterprise agentic workflows

**Important:** Very few agencies use the term "ReAct" directly in public marketing. In most cases below, agentic fit is inferred from explicit language around reasoning, planning, tools, orchestration, memory, and multi-step autonomous execution.

## Selection Criteria

Firms were evaluated on:

1. **Explicit agent architecture language** — multi-agent, orchestration, planning, tool use
2. **Production evidence** — demos, case studies, sandbox environments
3. **Differentiation from chatbot wrappers** — true autonomous agent systems vs. conversational AI reskin
4. **Public verifiability** — claims backed by visible product pages, not just marketing copy
